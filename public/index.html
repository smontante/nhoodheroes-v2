Our Mission

Neighborhood Heroes inc. was founded with the purpose of helping kids be the Heroes. With the help of our donors, our Non Profit will be able to provide opportunities for kids to impact the communities through; Donating to families in need, helping the homeless, creating back to school packages, hosting food drives and everything else our Heroes Want to accomplish. Our mission is to assist our youth in discovering new ways that they are able to make a positive impact in their neighborhoods. Neighborhood Heroes will provide a safe space for our youth to create change not only for their lives but to those around them.
